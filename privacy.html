English

Hairqoo ("we", "us") processes personal and business data as needed to provide the Hairqoo application and related services. This includes, where applicable: account and profile data; data related to salons, clients, appointments, bookings, and service history; content you choose to store (such as notes, formulas, or images); and technical data required to operate the service (for example device or browser information, logs, security signals, and diagnostics).

We use this information to run, secure, and improve the product, communicate with you about the service, and meet legal obligations where they apply.
